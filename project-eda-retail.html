Data Loading & Feature Engineering

Python
import pandas as pd
import numpy as np
import matplotlib.pyplot as plt
import seaborn as sns

df = pd.read_excel('Retail Sales Dataset.xlsx')
print(f"Shape: {df.shape[0]:,} rows x {df.shape[1]} columns")
# Output: Shape: 62,884 rows x 31 columns

# Feature engineering
df['LineTotal'] = df['Quantity'] * df['ProductPrice']
df['Profit'] = df['LineTotal'] - df['Quantity'] * df['ProductCost']
df['DeliveryDays'] = (df['DeliveryDate'] - df['OrderDate']).dt.days

Python
category_revenue = df.groupby('ProductCategory').agg(

Outlier Detection (IQR Method)

Python
for col in ['Quantity', 'ProductPrice', 'LineTotal', 'Profit']:
    Q1 = df[col].quantile(0.25)
    Q3 = df[col].quantile(0.75)
    IQR = Q3 - Q1
    lower = Q1 - 1.5 * IQR
    upper = Q3 + 1.5 * IQR

    outliers = df[(df[col] < lower) | (df[col] > upper)]
    print(f"{col}: {len(outliers)} outliers ({len(outliers)/len(df)*100:.1f}%)")

Key Findings

Revenue Insights

  • Total Revenue: $55,755,480 across 26,326 orders (Avg Order Value: $2,118)
  • Total Profit: $32,662,688 with an overall margin of 58.6%
  • Revenue peaked in 2019 at $18.3M before declining in 2020–2021

Category & Brand Performance

  • Computers dominate at $19.3M (34.6% of revenue), followed by Home Appliances ($10.8M)
  • Adventure Works is the top brand ($11.8M), followed by Contoso ($10.8M) and Wide World Importers ($9.2M)
  • Desktops subcategory alone generates $9.9M — 17.8% of total revenue

Geographic Distribution

  • United States leads with $29.9M (53.6%), followed by UK ($7.1M) and Germany ($5.4M)
  • 8 customer countries across North America, Europe, and Oceania
  • 58 stores across 9 locations including an Online channel

Channel & Customer Insights

  • Physical stores generate 79.5% of revenue; Online contributes 20.5%
  • Near-equal gender split: Male 50.8% ($28.3M) vs Female 49.2% ($27.4M)
  • 11,887 unique customers with avg 2.2 orders per customer

Seasonal Patterns

  • February ($7.8M) and December ($7.5M) are the strongest revenue months
  • April is the weakest month at just $607K — potential seasonal dip
  • Black, White, and Silver products account for 65% of total revenue

Data Quality & Delivery

  • DeliveryDate is 79.06% missing (49,719 transactions) — likely in-store pickups
  • For orders with delivery data: average 4.5 days (range: 1–17 days)
  • 2,492 unique products across 32 subcategories and 10 brands
